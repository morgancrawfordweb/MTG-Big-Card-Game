Archenemy Deck Builder

A 4-Player Pod Alternative

Rules

  • Team turns are shared.
  • Teams consist of one player(Archenemy) vs any number of players. Typically 1v3 or 2v5.
  • Scheme deck must be at least 20 cards. You can't have more than 2 of the same card.
  • Archenemy's life is 40 and other life is 20. For commander it should be 70/40.
  • Archenemy goes first.
  • You "Set a scheme in motion" immediately after the archenemy's precombat main phase.
